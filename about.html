About

MKE County Zoo Guide is a website that allows you to research the animals in the zoo before visiting. This site is about certain mammals in different habitats. The habitats include Adventure Africa, African Waterhole, Florence Mila Borchert Big Cat Country, North America, Northwestern Mutual Family Farm, and Primates of the world. This site currently only showcases three animals in each habitat. I wanted to create this website to make zoo visitors more aware of what they are going to see and how they can be better prepared.
